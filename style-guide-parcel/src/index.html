Style Guide 1.0

Purpose/Theme

We are planning to do a space theme; more specifically a nebula theme. Our background image would be of a nebula. The color scheme will consist of deep pinks, purples, & blue. We also think that use a lot of opacity and transparency will bring out the darkness of the background. For our preamble, we decided on making it seem like it has depth through space (with the use of opacity and depth, see wireframes as an example). Above all, we wanted our colors to match the background, displaying the deep pinks, purples, and blues. Overall, we want our theme to be dark.
